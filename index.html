The Blade Awaits.

The guillotine is heavy with the lingering presence of Charles-Henri Sanson. Even long after his death, the sheer intensity of his confliction; the shame of being an executioner clashing with his rigid loyalty to his State, remains imprinted on the machine. His essence acts as the invisible operator haunting the user with a weary, obligated hand, urging the user to enact their own sense of "justice".

The blade does not choose. That burden falls to you. Submit your image from your phone, and it will find its way beneath the machine. Sanson will do the rest.

The Experience

S

The Guillotine exists to provide a controlled space where users can dismantle the weight of unfair power dynamics. It is a world where the "barbaric" nature of emotional manipulation or social hierarchy is met with a "just" solution. Its purpose is to give the user a sense of reclaiming agency. By "equalizing" the image of someone who has caused frustration, the user feels a shift from being a victim of a dynamic to being the one who dictates its end.

T

Under the blade, any status or impact that the photo may have is stripped away. As the Guillotine seeks to equalize everything, it ensures that no ego or influence is immune to justice or accountability. The blade represents the severing of any influence over the user The user enacts their own justice through the blade.

O

The Guillotine acts as a kind of bridge between the digital and physical world, where users can submit digital evidence of their frustrations to be dealt with physically. This gives the use a bit of personal catharsis,

R

As designers, we are exploring the intersectionality of human design, specifically how a device born from a Revolutionary-era desire for equal justice can be adapted to serve a modern psychological need.

Y

The user hopes to see an "unfair" person or situation reduced to an equal, powerless state.The user uploads the image to our website, it is printed out by us and placed under the guillotine. Users move from feeling "less than" in a power dynamic to being the "equalizer" who decides when the tension is cut.
